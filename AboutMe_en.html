Personal information

I have been composing since 22 years old. It is all started as a simple fascination, but then transformed to the real calling. My professional activity started in 2000. It's hard to explain style I am working in. Maybe you will cope with this? My view is simple - music have to be simply liked. Does anyone are about the style when they hear music?
From the very beginning my music was English language orientated. English language is quite melodious. Of course it doesn't means that it can not be performed in Russian, but it happened so.
